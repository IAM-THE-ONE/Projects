Participation Guidelines

General Conduct

All participants are expected to maintain decorum and follow the college's code of conduct. Respectful behavior towards fellow participants and organizers is mandatory.

Registration Requirements

All participants must register through the official registration link. Ensure that you provide accurate information to avoid issues with entry.

Time Management

Please arrive at events 15 minutes before the scheduled start time. Late arrivals may not be allowed to participate.

Event-Specific Rules

Each event may have unique rules or guidelines. Participants are encouraged to review these details on the Events page and adhere to them strictly.

Prohibited Items

Items such as alcohol, drugs, and any other substances that violate college policy are strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
