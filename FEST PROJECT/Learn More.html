Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Swayam Fest?

All MVJCE students are welcome to participate. Select events may also be open to external participants.

How can I register for an event?

You can register for events on the registration page, accessible from the main navigation bar.

Is there a fee for participation?

Most events are free for MVJCE students. Some specialized workshops may have minimal fees.

Where is the fest held?

Swayam Fest takes place on the MVJCE Bengaluru campus. Venue details for each event will be provided closer to the date.
